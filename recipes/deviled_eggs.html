Deviled Eggs

Description

An easy to make appetizer

Ingredients

  • 6 large eggs hard-cooked
  • 1/4 cup mayonnaise
  • 1 teaspoon dijon mustard
  • 1 teaspoon cider vinegar

Steps

  1. Cut eggs in half and pop out yolks into a resealable food-safe plastic bag.
  2. Add remaining ingredients to the bag and mix together.
  3. Cut off a bottom corner of the bag and pipe the mixture into the egg whites.
