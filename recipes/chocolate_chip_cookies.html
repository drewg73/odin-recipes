Chocolate Chip Cookies

Description

Classic chocolate chip cookies.

Ingredients

  • Parchment Paper
  • 2 1/2 cups flour
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground cinnamon (Optional)
  • 1 cup butter softened
  • 1 cup packed brown sugar
  • 1/2 cup granulated sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • 2 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1 (12 ounce) bag semi-sweet chocolate chips

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350Â° F. Line a baking sheet with parchment paper and set aside.
  2. Combine flour, baking soda, salt, and cinnamon in a mixing bowl and set aside.
  3. Beat together butter, brown sugar and granulated sugar in a large mixing bowl until light and fluffy. Beat in eggs and vanilla.
  4. Beat together flower and butter mixtures until smooth and stir in the chocolate chips.
  5. Place balls of cookie dough (golfball sized) onto the baking sheet.
  6. Bake for 10 to 12 minutes or until lightly browned.
